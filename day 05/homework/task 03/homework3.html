dragon

In the first Shrek movie, Dragon is initially introduced as the guardian of Princess Fionas tower. Shes a fierce, fire-breathing dragon who keeps Fiona captive, but her role is more complex than just being a simple obstacle. She eventually becomes a key character in the series, adding humor, emotion, and even romance.

Character Traits:

Appearance: Dragon is a large, intimidating creature with red scales, powerful wings, and the ability to breathe fire. She’s formidable in battle but has a soft side that is revealed as her character develops. Personality: Although Dragon is initially presented as an antagonist (guarding Fiona’s tower), her personality is more complex. She’s shown to be strong, independent, and capable, but also gentle and loving as she becomes close to Donkey. Over time, she reveals her more affectionate and caring side.
